Corporate Information

Engineering Excellence Since Incorporation

BEKKS & BEE (NIGERIA) LIMITED is a company incorporated in Nigeria to render engineering consultancy, construction, project management, environmental studies and protection. It is duly registered and incorporated by the corporate affairs commission.

Our mission is clear: To become the best indigenous African company in rendering Engineering, Construction and Real Estate Services and to create and maintain effective and efficient work environment.

BEKKS & BEE (NIG) LIMITED is a 100% shareholding company with PETER BISONG as our Chief Executive Officer and Executive Director-Projects.

Health, Safety and Environment Policy

Statement of Quality

The management of the Project Quality Plan is the responsibility of the C.E.O of BEKKS & BEE (Nigeria) Limited.

  • BEKKS & BEE recognizes that a well-managed operation is more likely to be completed incident free and efficiently. HSE is considered as high on the agenda of our business objectives.
  • BEKKS & BEE is committed to establishing and maintaining effective system for managing the health, safety and environment impact of our operations.
  • Project employees, sub-contractors and any third parties from injury and prevent damage or loss of property.
  • It is the responsibility of all our managers to actively pursue an injury free work place.
  • BEKKS & BEE is committed to ensuring all BEKKS & BEE and contractor employees are competent and adequately trained to perform their task safely, and that each individual has a responsibility to do so.
  • BEKKS & BEE policy is that all incident or accident are reported without "blame" in order that they may be properly investigated and appropriate corrective actions can be taken.
  • The protection of life and prevention of injuries shall receive priority above everything else.
  • BEKKS & BEE shall co-operate with federal, state and local governments and any other relevant stakeholders in sharing/analyzing emerging HSE problems and develop cost effective, scientifically based HSE standards.

ENGR. PETER BISONG
C.E.O
